APPS - SECTION

Introducing TimeMaster, your personal productivity assistant! With TimeMaster, you have the power to optimize your screen time for maximum productivity. Simply enter the total time you want to allocate to unproductive activities, and let our intelligent scheduling algorithm handle the rest.

    HOW IT WORKS !



  • CLICK THE ADD BUTTON UPTO THE NUMBER OF APPLICATIONS AND SAY THE APPLICATION NAME IN THE PRIORITY OF YOU WANTED TO USE

  • ENTER THE TOTAL NUMBER OF MINUTES YOU WANTED TO SPEND UNPRODUCTIVELY

  • HIT THE GO - UNPRO BUTTON AFTER PROMPTING THE INPUTS

  • A LEADERBOARD WILL BE DISPLAYED WITH THE CALCULATED TIME FOR EACH APP


After inputting your preferences, TimeMaster will create a customized schedule, ensuring that your unproductive time is spent in a balanced and controlled manner. Stay focused, stay efficient with TimeMaster!
